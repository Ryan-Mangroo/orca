Getting Started

1. Setup your feedback form

You can configure your Inbox in the Settings menu at the top of the page. Then select the Inbox you want to setup.

Here you can change the description (not publicly visible), the public title and image.

This is also where you can preview the public form and get the link to share.

If you need to, you also have options for making the form inactive or resetting link.

2. Share your feedback form

After getting the link for your form, be sure to share it with your employees as a place for them to leave feedback.

You can preview the form for yourself at anytime by visiting the link in the Inbox settings page.

3. Setup your dashboard

After logging in, you'll see something like this.

This is because you don't have any feedback yet so our machine can't figure out the key issues at your company.

You can start by entering what you feel are important areas or topics at your organization.

Over time, our machine may change this depending on the feedback you get but it's a good start.
