Setting up your feedback form

By default, your inbox is given a generic name which you should change to reflect what you're using it for.

Some Examples:
"Employee feedback" or "Customer Satisfaction Survey"

Then, you'll want to change the public "Question" that is asked when users come to the form.

You can also change the image for your feedback form.
